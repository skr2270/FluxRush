# Privacy Policy for FluxRush Web

**Last Updated:** June 28, 2026

At **FluxRush**, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle browser camera permissions and local data when you play **FluxRush** on the web.

---

## 1. Camera Access & Usage
- **Purpose**: The web application requests browser permission to access your device camera. This permission is used exclusively to capture frames in real-time to detect hand landmarks (specifically your index finger, thumb, and knuckles) for gesture-based game controls.
- **On-Device Local Processing**: All camera processing is performed entirely **locally inside your browser** using MediaPipe's WebAssembly library. Camera frames are processed in-memory and are **never** saved, recorded, or written to your local file system.
- **No Transmission**: None of the video streams, frames, images, or tracking coordinates are ever transmitted over the network or shared with any external servers.

---

## 2. Information Collection and Storage
- **Personal Data**: The website does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
- **Local Storage**: Your high score and game preferences (e.g. quality settings, control modes) are saved locally in your browser's `localStorage`. This data is not uploaded anywhere and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser cache/storage.
- **Third-Party Services**: We do not use third-party analytics libraries, advertising trackers, or telemetry tools.
